Kora's platform allows brands to sign up and configure their product lines.

Product details—such as origin, materials, and other specifications are entered to generate a Digital Product Passport for each item.

Once ready, the brand requests unique IDs for each item (e.g., 1 product with 100 items = 100 IDs). Kora registers these IDs on the blockchain and assigns initial ownership to the brand.

Each item is physically tagged with its unique ID (such as a QR code), completing traceability.

Ownership is transferred to the consumer at the point of sale.

When resold through peer-to-peer channels or marketplaces, ownership is updated using Kora's user-friendly app.

Owners can scan the unique ID at any time to verify authenticity, access ownership history, and view the Digital Product Passport. Resale is always an option, repeating Step 6.
